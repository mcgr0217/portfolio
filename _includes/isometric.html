Home Theatre

Isometric Illustration

The illustration portrays the real life cinema experience but the setting is in a laptop to show that these days most people watch their movies at home on their laptops. The proportion is out of perspective to illustrate what the real life objects at home would be.

The illustration goes through a process and it starts with picking up the tickets for the movie.

While waiting for the movie you can go play games with your friends and hang out around the cinema.

You can pick up your drinks and snacks before viewing the movie.

When ready, you then go to wait in line to see the new release of a movie.
