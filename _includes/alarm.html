Alarm Buddy is the innovative solution for those who just can’t seem to get up on time, it’s a go to app for heavy sleepers!

TARGET AUDIENCE

Alarm Buddy is an app that targets a wide range of users anywhere aged from about 16 all the way to 45 with simple to moderate use of technology skills.

PAIN POINTS
Always pushing the snooze button.
Falling back asleep immediately.
Not feeling motivated to get up.
